## What helps us fix things quickly

- What you were trying to do, and what happened instead.
- A link to the candidate, [job](/jobs), or [sequence](/outreach) involved.
- A screenshot if it's visual.

## Feature requests

Very welcome. The most useful ones describe the problem — "I can't tell which sourced candidates my teammate already looked at" — rather than only a solution. It helps us fix the underlying thing, and you'll usually get it faster.
