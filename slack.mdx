Needle's agent works inside your Slack workspace: ask it for things in DMs or channels, and get hiring updates without opening the app.

## Connect

Connect your Slack workspace from **Settings → Integrations → Slack** — it installs the Needle app and links it to your organization. Most teams also pick (or create) a hiring channel for updates.

## Talk to the agent

DM the Needle app, or @mention it in any channel it's in. It handles the same things you'd do in the app:

- "How's the backend engineer [pipeline](/pipeline) looking?"
- "Draft a rejection email for Anna"
- "Move Tom to final round"
- "Open a senior designer [role](/jobs)"
- "Enroll the top 5 sourced backend candidates in the standard [sequence](/outreach)"

It answers with real data and links into the app when you want the full view.

## Updates that come to you

In your hiring channel, the agent posts what needs attention:

- New applicants with their [screening](/screening) results
- Summaries after interviews
- Replies from candidates in [outreach](/outreach)
- Sourced shortlists ready for review

## Approving drafts

When the agent drafts an email or interview invite, the draft shows up in Slack with the full text. Approve it there and it sends; edit or dismiss it if it's not right.

<Warning>
  Nothing outbound is sent without your sign-off — the same approval rules apply in Slack as in the app.
</Warning>
